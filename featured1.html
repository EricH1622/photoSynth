Alta Lake


After staying in whistler for several nights I finally got the perfect picture of the lake.


Location:

Whistler, British Columbia. On the other side of "Whistler on the Lake" Hotel at 4:15

Camera Setup:

I shot this on my Nikon n7 with Long Exposure NR: On, flicker reduction: On, and HDR off.
